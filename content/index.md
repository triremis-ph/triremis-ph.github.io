# TRIREMES PH

> **Trireme** (English) / **Triremes** (Latin) noun
> *an ancient galley with three banks of oars, popularized by the Ancient Greeks and Romans.*


Like the Greeks and the Romans sailed the open sea to fight wars, Triremes PH aims to sail the landscape of Philippine academia and tech industry. Unlike these ancient people, we have no wars to win. What we do have is a mission to champion: providing more regional representation and opportunities for students to grow outside their course syllabus. 

Triremes was founded with the premise that every Filipino university student in computer engineering, computer science, IT, and other tech-adjacent programs must be equipped with modern workflows and frameworks... And we mean every Filipino university student, not just the ones in Metro cities like Manila or Cebu, but also in provinces like of Negros Occidental and the greater western Visayas.

For the longest time, students groups--such as clubs and organizations, have been primarily contextualized in their respective universities here in Negros Occidental. While the status quo works well enough, the region is starved for a community where students can form interconnected webs and exchange insights, to foster a sense of camaraderie between peers outside ones' institution. Moreover, The concept of "building in public" comes off as a niche rather than the norm. 

Triremes is still in its embryonic stage, we will start as a very small friend group with a passion for working on technical projects, as well as being motivated to bring the banner of our region's pride and take a shot at hackathons. We hope to gather support from organizations like Google Developer Group Bacolod, ICpEP.se, and other circles to reach as many students as possible. By Q1 of 2027, we aim to join our first international hackathon. We want to make an example so that our peers can be inspired. We want to shatter the dogma that engaging with the international tech scene and contests is high-friction. We want to organize a community where we can coordinate and prepare for upcoming events and hackathons. We aim to raise a pocket of student developers who are confident in their abilities as we encourage each other to build and ship projects while still in the academia.

Triremes is a singular word in Latin. We believe that by starting local, we are able to concentrate our efforts into building something that sails. Triremes is a plural word in English. We believe that as the project grows, we welcome warmly a larger community with open arms. Either way, Triremes, true to its name, will be the vessel to make this vision into reality.
